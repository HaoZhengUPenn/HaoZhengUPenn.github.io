AIG建筑智能研究组（Architectural Intelligence Group）


汇聚人工智能和建筑跨界学术智库团队，突破设计与人工智能在交叉领域的学术研发与落地应用。团队汇集Cambridge、ETH、UCB、CMU、UCL、UPenn、NUS、清华、同济等多所高校学者与博士；具有多年从事创意设计、研究与开发的工作经验；融合建筑城规、人机交互、计算机、大数据等多领域跨界融合，基于人工智能，共同推进未来环境与生活的创新想法。

Gathering the cross-border academic brain tank team of artificial intelligence and architecture to break through the academic research and development and practical application of design and artificial intelligence in the cross-field. Our team brings together scholars from Cambridge, ETH, UCB, CMU, UCL, UPenn, NUS, Tsinghua, Tongji and other universities; has many years of work experience in creative design, research and development; integrates architectural urban planning, human-computer interaction, big data and other cross-border integration, based on artificial intelligence, to jointly promote innovative ideas for the future environment and life.

AIG深耕人工智能和建筑等相关领域的核心新兴技术、创新型学术思想。我们致力于寻找未来人工智能在建筑、城市、景观、建造等领域学术研究与技术应用的共同突破口，实现智能化创意设计与实现的新可能。AIG自成立以来，专注于人工智能、大数据、数字设计等新兴技术领域，融合前沿学术突破创新商业应用，基于跨界设计师视角，不断赋能行业与生活创意。基于研究和商业领域的科技经验，结合Technology+Design+Creation，AIG不断积累与更新自研可控的前沿数字创意技术与其智能化的生活应用场景。

AIG is deeply involved in core emerging technologies and innovative academic ideas in artificial intelligence, architecture and other related fields. We are committed to finding common breakthroughs in future academic research and technical applications of artificial intelligence in the fields of architecture, cities, landscapes, construction, etc., to realize new possibilities for intelligent creative design and implementation. Since its establishment, AIG has focused on emerging technology fields such as artificial intelligence, big data, and digital design, integrating cutting-edge academic breakthroughs and innovative commercial applications, and continuously empowering industry and life creativity based on the perspective of cross-border designers. Based on technological experience in research and business fields, combined with Technology+Design+Creation, AIG continues to accumulate and update self-developed and controllable cutting-edge digital creative technologies and intelligent life application scenarios.




郑豪教授（Prof. Dr. Hao ZHENG）


郑豪教授现任香港城市大学建筑学及土木工程学系助理教授，建筑学硕士项目副主任，建筑智能研究组主任。他博士毕业于宾夕法尼亚大学（University of Pennsylvania），专攻机器学习，生成式人工智能，数据驱动式设计，大数据分析。他于加州大学伯克利分校（UC Berkeley）获得建筑学硕士学位，于上海交通大学获得建筑学学士及文学学士学位。他曾工作于清华大学和加州大学伯克利分校，研究机械臂辅助施工，机器学习，仿生式3D打印。他的教学经验包括，同济大学工作营导师，宾夕法尼亚大学讲师，上海交通大学设计实践导师等等。同时，他还担任国际会议ACADIA、CAADRIA、CDRF的协办人和审稿人，以及30余本SCI/SSCI/AHCI期刊审稿人。他总计发表70余篇学术文章，其中包含20余篇SCI/SSCI/AHCI期刊文章以及Advanced Science封面文章。另外，郑豪博士主持开发和研究项目金额总计超过500万元人民币，并获得多项研究和教学奖项。他总计教授30余门次课程，指导10余位博士生，受邀在大型国际会议和学术组织上发言百余次，多次担任期刊主编、学会成员、上市公司专家顾问等等。

Prof. Dr. Hao Zheng currently serves as an Assistant Professor and the Associate Program Leader of the Master of Architecture Program at the Department of Architecture and Civil Engineering, City University of Hong Kong. He is also the director of the Architectural Intelligence Group (AIG). He graduated from the Ph.D. program at the University of Pennsylvania, specializing in machine learning, AI-generated content, data-driven design, and big data analysis. He holds a Master of Architecture degree from the University of California, Berkeley, and Bachelor of Architecture and Arts degrees from Shanghai Jiao Tong University. Previously, Hao worked as a research assistant at Tsinghua University and UC Berkeley with a concentration on robotic assembly, machine learning, and bio-inspired 3D printing. His teaching experience includes: workshop tutor at Tongji University; lecturer at the University of Pennsylvania; teaching fellow at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. Also, Hao serves as the co-organizer and reviewer for international conferences of ACADIA, CAADRIA, CDRF, and more than 30 SCI/SSCI/AHCI journals. His publication includes around 70 papers with more than 20 SCI/SSCI/AHCI journal articles and the cover paper in Advanced Science. In addition, Hao has led development and research projects with a total amount of 750,000 USD, and won multiple research and teaching awards. He has taught more than 30 courses, supervised more than 10 doctoral students, and been invited to give more than 120 speeches at international conferences and academic organizations. He also served as the editor for journals, the member of academic societies, and the expert consultant for listed companies, etc.
