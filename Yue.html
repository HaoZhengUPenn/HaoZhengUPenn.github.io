Yue Ren
March Architectural Design, University College London
B.A (Hons) in Interior Design, University of Edinburgh
B.A in Environment Design (Fashion Interior Design), Donghua University

Yue Ren is currently studying architectural design at UCL. She graduated from the ECA of the University of Edinburgh and SCF of Donghua University with the background of interior design and environment design. During her undergraduate study, Yue Ren’s urban and architecture works participated in various exhibitions like 2018 Beijing International Design Week, 2017 Sino-Finnish Challenge Exhibition and 2016 Shanghai “Drawing is an active of thinking” Exhibition. Her interior graduation project was recommended by Pro. Ed Hollis for attending 2019 London Free Range to represent the ECA. With both experience of the practical and the conceptual, Yue Ren also has the digital architecture experience in machine learning and mixed reality in 2019 Digital Future and 2018 CAADRIA.
