AIG

Architectural Intelligence Group


Architectural Intelligence Group (AIG) is an open information exchange community platform that shares the latest research on artificial intelligence in architectural design. Team members can obtain information shared within the group on the platform and publish their own papers.
建筑智能研究组是一个开放的信息交流社区平台，分享人工智能在建筑设计上的最新研究成果。组员可以在平台上获取组内共享的信息，并发布自己的论文。

With the rapid development of Artificial Intelligence, the applications of machine learning, especially based on the neural network, grow as if in the spring after the rain. Architecture, the very end application of technology, finally joins the family of AI, architectural researchers are passionate to apply or develop different kinds of neural networks in solving design problems. There is no need for more words to emphasize that machine learning has become a new and growing field in architectural design. As long as there is data, machine learning can be used to find the laws and rules behind it, and further produce similar data according to the learning.
随着人工智能的快速发展，机器学习的应用，尤其是基于神经网络的机器学习，如同雨后春笋一般。建筑学作为技术的末端应用，终于加入了AI家族，建筑师们热衷于应用或开发不同类型的神经网络来解决设计问题。显然，机器学习已成为建筑设计中一个新兴的领域。只要有数据，机器学习就可以用来找到它背后的法律和规则，并根据学习进一步产生类似的数据。

However, besides the techniques, under the power of AI, discussions are proposed, such as “Can AI replace human and become a new architect?” and “If yes, what will be the future for human architect?” Through the information sharing of the applications of machine learning in architectural design, AIG discusses a future that, is it possible that the computer may not only be an assistant, but also be a decision maker to do creative jobs, and finally replace mental workers, like architects? So nowadays, with the rapid improvement of AI technology, our architects should think about our own positions, find new values and breakthrough points, and defense ourselves from the incoming impact. Future, humans, architects, will be the issues we need to discuss.
然而，除了技术层面，在AI的高速发展下，一些讨论涌现了出来，例如“人工智能可以取代人类并成为新的建筑师吗？”“如果是的话，人类建筑师的未来会如何？”AIG将持续关注人工智能在建筑领域应用发展，来探讨一种未来的可能：计算机是否能不仅作为辅助者，而是作为决策者，取代建筑师呢？在人工智能高速发展的今天，我们建筑师应该思考自己的定位，找到合适的价值观和突破点，应对即将到来的冲击。未来，人类，建筑师，这将是我们之后需要探讨的问题。
